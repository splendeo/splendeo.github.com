h2. "Who we are":/en/who-we-are/

Splendeo Innovación is a software development and technological consultancy company. Its objective is boldly solving some of the problems business process problems derived from the information management.

Our phylosophy has two big pilars: agile, talented development and use of Open Source Software (OSS)

Currently we have engaged "Madrid Emprende":http://madridemprende.esmadrid.com/ , an initiative of the Madrid City Council for innovative companies.
